About Tiago Santos (Founder)

I have a degree in Systems Information and over 10 years of experience in system developing, 8 of these years using .NET, ASP.NET, MVC versions 4/5, C #, Azure platform, WEBSERVICE. I have worked with Oracle, MS Sql Server, MySql and pgsql data base. I also have experience with SAP integration, Java, j2ee, struts, springs, hibernate, PHP, ASP 3.0, Integration Service (ETL).

In these companies, I collaborated in national-scale systems in government ministries, Brazilian agencies and Brazilian telecommunications companies.
